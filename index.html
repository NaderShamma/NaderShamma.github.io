Research: Utilising Mobile Health and Smart Technology to Aid with the Treatment and Management of Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD)

How you will be helping:

By filling out this survey you will help us get a much better understanding of who IBD patients are, what challenges they face and how we can help. Furthermore we hope to use this information in addition to information gathered from other studies to develop a virtual patient to simulate someone with IBD so that we may fully test any application that is being built.

createTAKE PART

  • Purpose of the research

    Our aim is to understand how and if mobile health (smartphones and smart wearables) can be utilised to help outpatients better manage their illness. Our objective is to develop a system that not only helps patients track their condition but also offers them decision support when it comes to managing their health.

  • Purpose of the survey:

    This survey has been developed to try and better understand you, the patient. We want to understand how the illness is affecting you, how you currently self manage your illness, any challenges you face and what features you as a patient would find most beneficial from an application developed to help you manage your condition. The survey shouldn't last more than 15 to 20 minutes.

  • Is this survey anonymous?

    You will be undertaking this survey with complete anonymity. The survey asks for no personal identifiable information.

    Please Note: I will be looking for volunteers to provide feedback on any prototype built, if you would like to participate then you will be asked to leave an email at the end to receive more info. I promise I won't Spam you and your details will be kept confidential. This is entirely optional and please know you are not obliged to participate in the future. Thank you.

  • Who can participate in this survey?

    We are inviting any adult 18 years old and over with a confirmed diagnosis of IBD (either Crohn's Disease or Ulcerative Colitis) to participate in the research.

  • Who is conducting this research?

    My name Nader, I am a Computing Systems MSc student at Nottingham Trent University, a software engineer and a fellow IBD sufferer having lived with Crohn's Disease for over 10 years. My aim is to build a smart app to help other IBD patients, like myself, better manage their illness so they can live a comfortable life in remission for longer and with far fewer flareups.

  • What will happen with the results of this survey?

    The results of the research will be primarily used and published within an academic MSc thesis and potentially academic publications such as conference and journal papers or other such media articles.

  • Can I withdraw my entry?

    Yes. Even though you are answering this survey anonymously we will ask two special questions which you can use to identify yourself if you wish to withdraw (see section 3). These questions include "Enter the name of a memorable childhood movie." and "Enter the name of a memorable city, town or place." If you wish to withdraw simply email us via nader.al-shamma022015[at]my.ntu.ac.uk with the answer to those two questions and you will be removed from the survey results. Please note you must state that you wish to withdraw before 14th August 2017 to ensure you information can be removed before work is submitted.

  • Who can I contact if I have any questions?

    If you have any questions about the research and would like to know more please contact Nader via email at nader.alshamma022015[at]my.ntu.ac.uk
