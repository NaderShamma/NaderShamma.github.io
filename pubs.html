Exploring the potential in utilising smartphones to aid in the treatment and management of inflammatory bowel disease

N. Al-Shamma and A. J. Nottingham, 2016 Future Technologies Conference (FTC), San Francisco, CA, 2016, pp. 696-700.

Abstract:

I am a Computing Systems MSc student at Nottingham Trent University and a software engineer with a passion for technology. My current research is focused on how to help people with chronic illnesses better self manage their condition in the hope that they can have a better quality of life.
