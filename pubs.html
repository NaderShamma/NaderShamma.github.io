Research: Utilising Mobile Health and Smart Technology to Aid with the Treatment and Management of Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD)

How you will be helping:

By filling out this survey you will help us get a much better understanding of who IBD patients are, what challenges they face and how we can help. Furthermore we hope to use this information in addition to information gathered from other studies to develop a virtual patient to simulate someone with IBD so that we may fully test any application that is being built.
